Help Center


  • What happens when I ask a new question or post an answer on Nesa Academy

    • If you have less than 50 reputations your question or answer would be submitted as a draft (but can be edited in your dashboard) and then reviewed by admin. If there are no errors, it would be published. By the way, you also get additional two points for your reputation

  • What happens when my question or answer gets approved

    • It would be posted live. By the way, you also get additional three points for your reputation.

  • What happens when I receive a message from admin

    • You are required to respond within 24 hours to the query.

  • What happens if my question or answer gets deleted by admin

    • Your drafted question or answer gets deleted permanently if you do not comply with the instruction given within 24 hours. Also, you get a decrease in reputation (-2).

  • How to ask a question

    1. Only Economics related questions are allowed with a blend of Statistics, Mathematics, Econometrics, and Finance questions
    2. Give your question a title (maximum of 100 characters)
    3. Explain your question in detail in the body section
    4. Add tags to your question (for example microeconomics, econometrics, etc)

    For example:

    Why is it that a monopoly can influence price or quantity and not both (This can be the title)
    Monopoly has the power to influence price and quantity and not both, please I would love to know the reason why (This can be the body)
    microeconomics, monopoly (This can be the tag(s))

    Please try to re-read the question to ensure there are no typographical errors
    Such questions would be approved quickly

  • How to answer a question

    1. You can only have one answer per question

    Please try to re-read the answer to ensure their are no typographical errors
    Such answers would be approved quickly

  • Why can't I delete a question that as an approved answer even though I was the one that asked the question

    • It can't be deleted because other users have taken time to answer your question. By the way, only users with over 100 reputations can delete an answer. Trust must be gained by a user to avoid malicious behavior from users

  • How to edit a question or answer

    • Only people with over 50 reputations can edit a question or an answer. Trust must be gained by a user to avoid malicious behavior from users

  • Why are my questions or answers or article not yet published

    • They would be published within 24 hours of posting given there are no queries with the posts

  • What is an Anonymous User

    • Questions, Answers, and Articles are tagged as an anonymous user showing that the original user has deleted his/her account

    • It is recommended to share your questions or articles with your peers or whoever can answer your question, or would benefit from it. You can share on all major social media platforms via the Nesa Academy Platform.
